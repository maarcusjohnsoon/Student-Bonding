About Student Bonding

Creating safe spaces for meaningful connections

Mission & What We Do

Student Bonding was created to foster authentic connections among college and university students through shared experiences and open dialogue. We believe that meaningful relationships are built on understanding, respect, and honest communication.

Our platform centers around the Rice Purity Testâ€”not as a judgment tool, but as a conversation starter that helps students explore their experiences, values, and boundaries in a supportive environment. We've reimagined this classic test to prioritize safety, consent, and inclusivity.

We understand that college years are a time of growth, exploration, and self-discovery. Our goal is to provide a space where students can navigate these experiences together, learning from each other while maintaining respect and understanding.

Every interaction on our platform is designed to build bridges, not barriers. We celebrate diversity of experiences while promoting healthy decision-making and emotional well-being among our community members.

Safety & Respect Guidelines

Our Community Standards

  • Consent First: All participation must be voluntary. Never pressure anyone to take the test or share results.
  • Privacy Matters: Respect others' privacy. Don't share or discuss someone else's results without permission.
  • No Shaming: Every score and experience is valid. We maintain a judgment-free environment.
  • Age Requirements: Our platform is designed for college-aged students (18+) only.
  • Mental Health Support: If discussions trigger concerns, we encourage seeking support from campus counseling services.
  • Respectful Discussion: Keep conversations respectful, inclusive, and supportive of all participants.
  • Boundaries: It's always okay to decline participation or step away from conversations.
