Additional Resources

If you're experiencing a mental health crisis or need immediate support, please reach out to these resources:

Campus Counseling Services

Most colleges offer free, confidential counseling services for students.

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741 for free, 24/7 crisis support.

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

Call or text 988 for immediate support.
